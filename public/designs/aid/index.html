Vector Embedding

Numeric coordinates that place words, sentences, or documents inside a semantic space where distance maps to meaning. Similar concepts land near each other; unrelated ones drift apart.

Definition
Concept Core
Vector embeddings encode data into dense numerical representations that preserve semantic relationships. They are the substrate beneath search, clustering, recommendation, retrieval, and similarity workflows across modern AI systems.

If “doctor” and “physician” sit close together while “banana” stays far away, the model has captured semantic relation rather than just exact token overlap.
